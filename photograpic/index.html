Photograpic Club

What we do....

The Photography Society of SRM University-AP, Andhra Pradesh, was founded on January 10, 2018,
with the objective of providing a supportive environment for students to learn, share, and grow their passion for photography.
The society welcomes students of all levels of experience and skill, and
encourages them to explore the art of photography through various activities and events.
