E-Sports

What we do....

The e-sports club of SRM University-AP, Andhra Pradesh is an excellent platform for our
talented students to take a break from their learning schedule and indulge in a relaxing fun time while
enhancing their cognitive skills. The club enables students to play video games at their leisure,
interact with their fellow mates, and show their skills in competitions organized frequently.
