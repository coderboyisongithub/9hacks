About Us

Clubs for music, dance, art, hobbies and sports are entrenched in the University campus that enables young minds to invest their time beyond academics. Each club is furnished with trained staff who bring out the best in students and find ways to engage them in activities after college hours. The learners are cheered to participate and win accolades in all art forms and are celebrated to have brought in accolades for the college. The campus is home to a diverse set of extra and co-curricular activities that help advance one's academic and non-academic pursuits.
