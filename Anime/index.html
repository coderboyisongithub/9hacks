What we do....

The Anime Society is a student organization dedicated to fostering a
welcoming and inclusive environment for individuals who enjoy watching anime and manga,
as well as western animated content. Our goal is to provide a safe and
enjoyable space where people can connect, share their interests, and grow as fans.
